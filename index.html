About

Who am I?

Yo! My name is Pedro Arthur. I started my career as a Graphic Designer during my time studying at UFRJ. While eager to learn more, at the end of my studies, I decided to embark on a new journey, taking a full turn to coding. I began studying by myself, and as I was falling in love with code, I made the decision to join the Le Wagon coding bootcamp, from where I came out fulfilled and confident to say that I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer.
Amateur illustrator for fun, and experienced with social & digital media work. I'm a geek, gamer, coffee lover, passionate about music, and a big trending topics follower.

Javascript and Ruby on Rails adept, I already worked with Laravel and PHP, and I'm currently developing my ReactJS skills. Always ready to learn more and grow as a person, improving life through code.
