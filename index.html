Yo, I'm Pedro!

I started my career as a Graphic Designer and Visual Communicator while studying at UFRJ, one of the best universities in Latin America and around the world. While eager to learn more and on a search for a way to keep up with the rest of the world, at the end of my studies, I decided to embark on a new journey, taking a full turn to coding and programming. I began studying by myself, and as I was falling in love with the code, I made the decision to join a 9-week intense coding bootcamp at Le Wagon Rio, where I was immersed in HTML, CSS, Javascript & Ruby on Rails. From that experience I came out fullfilled, confident to say that I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer.

Amateur illustrator, and experienced with social & digital media. Geek, gamer, and a big "trending topics" follower. I became experienced in the areas of graphic design and web development throughout the years, being a digital content creator on my own personal projects and on blogs about digital entertainment and pop culture.
Always ready to learn and grow as a person and professional, improving life through code.
